Offering a Full Range of Tool & Die Services

Die Makers Manufacturing has an experienced and talented team of designers, toolmakers, and machinists.  In addition our high-tech machine tools and equipment contribute to the success of our customers. We provide a full range of services including stamping die design and building.  We have presses in house so we can try out your die before it leaves our facility.  In addition we provide support services such as die maintenance, die repair, wire EDM, and CNC machining.  We also provide general machining. From design to completion, all work is performed under our comprehensive quality system assuring on-time delivery of high-quality products.

Die Makers Manufacturing Offers You:

  • State-of-the-art machines and equipment for maximum efficiency.
  • Highly skilled team of designers, machine operators, and tool and die makers.
  • Competitive prices and excellent delivery. Short lead times are not a problem.

Mission & Values

Die Makers is a complete manufacturing solution provider with a passion to commit and the conviction to deliver.  We strive to provide the very best end product in accordance to our customers’ precise specifications. Our customers are our partners.  Therefore we collaborate with them to deliver innovative solutions to manufacturing problems and optimize processes. Honesty, ingenuity, and hard work are just a few of the core values that the Die Makers Family has ingrained into our daily operations.

Our Story

Die Makers Manufacturing opened its doors in January of 1994.  We were first located in a 3,500 square feet facility in Kieler, WI.

In 1997 we moved to a 15,000 square feet facility in Hazel Green, WI.  Today our facility is 30,000 square feet and we have 25 employees.  We are family owned and operated.
